Rules

1. Team Size

Teams can consist of 3 to 5 participants. Collaborating in teams is required.

2. Eligibility

Participants must be officially registered for the project and comply with all event guidelines.

3. Original Work

Your submission must be your own and must not infringe upon the intellectual property rights of others.

4. Inclusivity

The event fosters a positive, inclusive, and respectful environment. Any form of offensive behavior will not be tolerated.

5. Collaboration and Help

Plagiarism or copying entire projects from other teams is strictly prohibited. Please reach out to mentors or officers for guidance and support at any point during the project cycle.

FAQs

What if I'm new to coding?

That's okay! This is a beginner-friendly project. Mentors will be available to help.

What is the time commitment for this?

You will need to be available for the weekly meetings with your mentor, the midpoint and the final presentation. Besides this, it depends on you and your team.

I don't have a team yet. How can I find one?

We will match people without teams at the Kickoff Meeting.

How will official communication occur during the project cycle?

We will use Discord to communicate with participants.
